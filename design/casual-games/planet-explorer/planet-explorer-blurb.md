# Planet Explorer

Category: Casual Games

A no-fail 2D side-scrolling exploration game set on a single, procedurally generated world. Each biome has its own adventurer — a Safari Explorer on the grasslands, a Diver on the reef, a Mountaineer in the highlands, and more. Switch between biomes whenever you like and discover the animals and minerals unique to each region. Track everything you touch in your field book, and unearth special Movement Abilities that give all your adventurers new ways to get around.
