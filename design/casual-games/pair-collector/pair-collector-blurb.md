# Pair Collector

Category: Casual Games

A sharp-eye card game where one card in the spread is duplicated — and it's your job to find both copies before time slips away. Each round shows a fresh layout of playing cards: almost all unique, but one appears exactly twice. Tap both copies to score the round. Three strikes and you're out, or finish all ten rounds for a final time score. How fast can you spot the pair?
