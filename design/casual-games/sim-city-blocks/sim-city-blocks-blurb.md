# Sim City Blocks

Category: Casual Games

A city-builder where you call the shots but the city does the planning. Buy zones, build civic buildings, and invest in upgrades — your town handles the layout. Start with a humble dirt-road settlement and a railroad spur, then grow it into a thriving city by balancing jobs, residents, infrastructure, and energy. Keep the books in the black, residents employed, and the city running, or watch your population pack up and leave. Random disasters keep you on your toes.
