# Idle Combat Training

A ninja steps up to the training dummy. He doesn't know much — but he's willing to practice.

Watch him stumble, miss, and slowly find his rhythm. With enough repetition he lands hits more consistently, unlocks new strikes, and learns to chain moves together. Each destroyed dummy earns cash, but the next one is tougher. Train long enough and your clumsy student becomes a master.

**Practice makes perfect. Keep swinging.**
