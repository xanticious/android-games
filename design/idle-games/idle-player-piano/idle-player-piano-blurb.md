# Idle Player Piano

The piano plays. At first, it plays nonsense.

But you'll keep listening — because every now and then it stumbles onto the target sequence, and that earns you cash. Upgrade the piano to nudge its randomness toward something more musical: a slight preference for the right note, then a stronger one, then something almost intentional. As you improve the instrument, the target sequences grow longer and the rewards grow richer.

**From noise to music, one upgrade at a time.**
