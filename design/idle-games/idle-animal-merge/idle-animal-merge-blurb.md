# Idle Animal Merge

Every hour, a new animal wanders into your grassland. If it matches one already there, you can merge them into something greater.

With 100 animals across 10 tiers, there's always a new creature to discover. You start seeing only humble Tier 1 arrivals, but as higher-tier animals populate your field, the odds shift and rarer visitors begin to appear. How high can you build your menagerie?

**Check in. Merge. Discover. Repeat.**
