# Idle Bounce

A bouncy ball digs its way to the center of the earth — one bounce at a time.

Watch your ball slam into layers of dirt, stone, ore, and bedrock. Every hit chips away at the ground below. When a layer is destroyed, you pocket the earnings and the ball bounces deeper. Spend your money to upgrade the ball and dig faster, harder, and further than ever before.

**Simple. Satisfying. Endless.**
