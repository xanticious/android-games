# Idle Farmers

From a single farmer scratching at dirt to a planet-spanning robotic harvest empire — witness 10,000 years of agriculture unfold.

Start with one farmer, one crop, and barely enough to eat. Gradually unlock barley, irrigation, crop rotation, selective breeding, chemistry, automation, and beyond. Upgrades depend on each other, mirroring the real chain of human agricultural discovery. There's always another breakthrough just around the corner.

**Watch humanity grow — one harvest at a time.**
