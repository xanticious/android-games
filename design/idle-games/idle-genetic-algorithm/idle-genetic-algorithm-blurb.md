# Idle Genetic Algorithm

Evolution doesn't need you — but your wallet helps.

Watch a genetic algorithm breed, mutate, and improve a fleet of cars on procedurally generated tracks. Each generation learns from the last, pushing further and faster. Earn passive income from survivors and collect bonuses when a car reaches a checkpoint. Reinvest your earnings into better parts — more fuel, a stronger engine, higher top speed, shielding — then sit back as the algorithm rediscovers greatness with the upgraded hardware.

**You fund the experiment. Evolution does the rest.**
