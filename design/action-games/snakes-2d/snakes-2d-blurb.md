# Snakes 2D

Category: Action Games

A reimagined snake game with full 2D directional movement. Tap anywhere on the screen to set your snake's destination — it glides in a straight line toward that point at any angle, not just 90-degree turns. Collect food to grow longer, but avoid the walls and your own ever-lengthening body. Smooth controls make movement feel intuitive, but the challenge grows with every bite.
