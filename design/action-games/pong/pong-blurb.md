# Pong

Category: Action Games

A high-definition reimagining of Pong as a bat-and-ball game. The ball bounces off the top and bottom walls in wallyball style — your job is to stop it from hitting your side wall. Tap where you want your bat to swing, and use the forehand/backhand toggle to change your swing arc. Time your strikes to send the ball back across the net. After each swing, a brief cooldown keeps you on your toes.
