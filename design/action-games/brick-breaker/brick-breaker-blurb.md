# Brick Breaker

Category: Action Games

A modern turn-based brick breaker where you fire a cannon loaded with 20 balls per turn to destroy falling bricks before they reach the bottom. Choose your launch position and angle each turn, then watch the physics play out. Bricks display their remaining hit points, and special bricks drop power-ups — from screen-clearing blasts to multi-shot bonuses — that change the tide in an instant.
