# Asteroids

Category: Action Games

A reimagined twin-stick shooter with high-definition graphics and updated gameplay. Pilot your spaceship through treacherous asteroid fields using a left-thumb joystick for movement and tap-to-aim shooting on the right. Each level tasks you with collecting five beacons scattered across the field — each beacon explodes on contact, damaging all nearby asteroids. Survive the chaos and collect every beacon to advance.
