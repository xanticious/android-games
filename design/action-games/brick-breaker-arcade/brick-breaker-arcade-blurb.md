# Brick Breaker (Arcade)

Category: Action Games

A real-time arcade twist on Brick Breaker where bricks slowly descend and you fire a continuous stream of balls upward to destroy them before they reach the bottom. Slide left and right to reposition your cannon and grab power-ups from destroyed bricks to unleash explosions, multi-shot, and more. Fast, frantic, and always one bad move away from disaster.
