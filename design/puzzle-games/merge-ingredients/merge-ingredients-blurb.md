# Merge Ingredients

Category: Puzzle Games

You start with a well stocked kitchen. Combine ingredients together to learn new all of the recipes.

Yeast + Flour = Dough
Dough + Oven = Bread
Tomato + Stove = Tomato Sauce
Tomato Sauce + Sugar + Salt = Ketchup
Bread + Tomato Sauce + Cheese + Salami = Pizza
