# Match Three

Category: Puzzle Games

A relaxed, endless gem-swapping game inspired by Bejeweled Zen. Swap adjacent tiles to line up three or more matching colors or shapes. New gems drop in from above to keep the board fresh. No timer, no pressure — just the satisfying click of a good match and the pleasure of watching chains cascade. If no moves are available, the board reshuffles automatically until at least one match is possible again.
